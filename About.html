Producer

Hello, welcome to my About page! My name is Mees Wiegmink, also known as Meezzz. I'm a 17-year-old DJ and producer from the Netherlands. Even though, my first official releases started in 2020, I've been producing Dance music for over seven years. My sound is influenced by Tech House, Bass House and Dubstep. However, most of my tracks are a mix of multiple genres. I try not to follow the latest trends in Dance music because I want to keep enjoying the process of building a track from scratch.

Kunstbende

25-06-2023
Meezzz wins 1st prize Kunstbende Noord-Brabant. I'm heading to the finals in the Melkweg in Amsterdam! Kunstbende is a (DJ) contest, organized in the Netherlands for people under 18 years old. After last years DJ-set, I've been trying to improve my skills as a DJ. This gave me the opportunity to play more gigs and parties, which made it all the more exciting to play my new set at this years edition of Kunstbende. I'm very excited to see what's to come, and I can't wait for the finals in Amsterdam!
